# Lint Checklist

Run this periodically to keep the wiki coherent.

## Consistency

- Are there duplicate pages for same concept/entity?
- Do key pages disagree without a "Contradictions / Tensions" note?

## Freshness

- Are major claims superseded by newer sources?
- Does `wiki/overview.md` reflect latest evidence?

## Connectivity

- Any orphan pages with no inbound links?
- Any pages with weak cross-linking opportunities?

## Coverage

- Frequently mentioned terms missing dedicated pages?
- Important open questions missing from `wiki/tasks/backlog.md`?

## Navigation

- Is `wiki/index.md` up to date with all created/renamed pages?
- Do section summaries still reflect page contents?
